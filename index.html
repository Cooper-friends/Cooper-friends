BENEFITS OF PET SITTING

  • Reduced Risk: Lowered exposure to kennel cough and other infections.
  • Home Comfort: Pets stay in their own home where they feel safe and secure.
  • Solo Care: Dogs are walked individually, not in groups, to ensure safety.
  • Routine Maintenance: Regular walks and playtime occur in the routine they are used to.

CANCELLATION POLICY

Walks can be rearranged or cancelled up to 24 hours before your visit without charge.

Notice of less than 24 hours incurs the full cost of your walk.

**Safety Priority: No charge for extreme weather cancellations.**
