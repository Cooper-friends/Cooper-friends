CANCELLATION POLICY

At Cooper and Friends, we understand life sometimes gets in the way. Therefore, walks can be rearranged or cancelled up to 24 hours before your scheduled walk or pet sit visit without charge.

If you contact us with less than 24 hours notice, we will have to charge the full cost of your walk.

As a small business, we cannot afford to lose out on income for these walks, so please let us know in plenty of time for any cancellations.

**There will be no charge for extreme weather cancellations as dogs safety is a priority.**
