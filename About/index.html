About Lisa

With over 25 years of experience in professional pet care, Lisa brings a lifetime of passion and expertise to Cooper & Friends.

Lisa specializes in solo dog walking, ensuring that your pet receives 100% of her attention and care during their time together. She understands that every dog is unique and provides a tailored experience to meet their individual energy levels and temperaments.

Beyond the Walk

In addition to solo walks, Lisa offers comprehensive house visits for animals of all shapes and sizes. Her experience extends far beyond dogs and cats; Lisa is highly skilled in caring for specialist pets, including snakes and other reptiles.

Whether your family includes a senior dog, a high-energy puppy, or a delicate reptile, you can trust Lisa to provide the professional, reliable, and loving care they deserve.
